Alasdair Pettigrew

Professional Profile - Leadership in Strategy, Transformation & Growth

With 35 years of experience spanning technology strategy, corporate transformation, and venture-backed growth, Alasdair Pettigrew has built and scaled businesses at the intersection of innovation and commercialisation. His career includes 22 years in the corporate world —working with BAE Systems in military aerospace engineering and IBM as a technology consultant — followed by 13 years as an entrepreneur, leading companies through digital transformation, SaaS growth, and go-to-market execution.

Alasdair believes that true, sustainable business transformation is only possible when organisations balance people, process, and technology effectively. He has successfully applied this philosophy across cloud software, AI, ML, data-driven strategy, and agile enterprise adoption, helping companies streamline operations, optimise workflows, and accelerate scalable growth.

As the former CEO of BOXARR (and still a shareholder), a deep-tech SaaS AI company, he led engagements with Airbus, Boeing, Raytheon, and the US NAVY, demonstrating how data analytics and AI-powered decision-making can unlock efficiency in complex systems. Today, Alasdair leverages his deep industry knowledge and extensive network of investors, accelerators, and strategic partners to help technology businesses, SaaS startups, and digital-first enterprises refine their strategic direction, secure funding, and scale effectively in highly competitive markets.
